With our following presentation, we will give you a more advanced understanding of baseball statistics of professional baseball players playing in the MLB.

In the traditional baseball, pitchers and batters were considered as two separate components of the game. Therefore, it was difficult to compare pitchers and batters in their worth and performances. In addition, typical stats like Earned Run Average (ERA) for pitchers and batting average (AVG) for batters don't always tell the whole picture for each respective player.

THEN CAME WAR:

WAR (Winning Above Replacement), provides a homogeneous metric for evaluating both pitchers and batters' performances at the same time. This statistic, along with with advanced performance variables such as wOBA and FIP, allows for us to gain a more accurate representation of the best players in Major League Baseball. These will be discussed in more detail below.
